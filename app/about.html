About PlantCare AI

Advanced Deep Learning for Agriculture AI Solutions

Our Mission

PlantCare AI is dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture through artificial intelligence. Our plant disease detection system empowers farmers and gardeners with instant, accurate diagnosis using cutting-edge deep learning technology.

By leveraging the power of MobileNetV2 and the PlantVillage Dataset, we provide real-time leaf disease detection that helps protect crops and ensure food security.
